People / Partner

Angela Ekene Anekwe, Esq.

Leads the commercial & corporate practice on a single principle: every agreement is eventually read by someone looking for a way out of it, so draft for that reader.

About

Angela advises founders, boards and family businesses on incorporation and governance under CAMA 2020, shareholders’ and founders’ agreements, commercial contracts and regulatory compliance. Her review memoranda are written for the director who must act on them, not only the lawyer who will file them.

She treats the shareholders’ agreement as the constitution a business will one day be judged by, and drafts its deadlock, exit and valuation machinery to work on the worst day, not just the first.
